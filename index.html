Location: FRI, Dehradun

The Forest Research Institute is a Natural Resource Service training institute of the Indian Council of Forestry Research and Education and is an institution in the field of forestry research in India for Indian Forest Service cadres and all State Forest Service cadres.

Location: Qutb Minar, Delhi

The Qutub Minar, also spelled as Qutb Minar and Qutab Minar, is a minaret and "victory tower" that forms part of the Qutb complex. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in the Mehrauli area of South Delhi, India.

Location: College Campus

The Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University (DBUU) is a non-profit, self-governed private University that runs several colleges in the field of Engineering, Computer Application, Architecture, Design (B.Des), Management, Commerce, Hotel Management, Mass Communication, BAMS, Fashion Designing, Agriculture, Polytechnic, Horticulture, Microbiology, Nursing, and Pharmacy in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. These institutes are approved by AICTE and are affiliated with the state Universities of Uttarakhand.
